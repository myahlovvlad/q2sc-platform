### About me

I design verifiable software and knowledge systems for scientific instruments, laboratories, and research-intensive products. My work connects measurement physics, analytical chemistry, software architecture, finite-state machines, UX, metrology, validation, technical documentation, and AI-assisted development.

My strongest role is at the boundary between domains: I translate scientific and regulatory requirements into explicit data models, workflows, acceptance criteria, interfaces, test scenarios, and working software prototypes.

I define the domain model, product logic, requirements, validation strategy, and acceptance criteria. Software implementation is developed through an AI-assisted engineering workflow and reviewed for scientific, architectural, and functional consistency.

## Educational case studies / Образовательные кейсы

The educational series explains computational chemistry through reproducible stories rather than isolated formulas. Every notebook is designed around a scientific question, a transparent model, executable code, interpretation, and model limitations.

Образовательная серия объясняет вычислительную химию через воспроизводимые истории: научный вопрос → модель → исполняемый код → интерпретация → ограничения метода.

### Available notebook / Доступный блокнот

**[From a benzene ring to molecular orbitals: a Hückel-model story](education/notebooks/01_benzene_huckel_story.ipynb)**  
**[От бензольного кольца к молекулярным орбиталям: модель Хюккеля](education/notebooks/01_benzene_huckel_story.ipynb)**

The notebook builds a simple π-electron Hamiltonian for benzene, calculates orbital energies and coefficients, identifies HOMO and LUMO levels, and explains what a minimal model can and cannot predict.

Блокнот строит простую матрицу гамильтониана π-системы бензола, рассчитывает энергии и коэффициенты орбиталей, определяет HOMO и LUMO и показывает границы применимости упрощённой модели.
